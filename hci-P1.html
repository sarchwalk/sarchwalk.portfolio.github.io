Acaikei and Horse Shoe Key

CPSC 581 - Human Computer Interaction II, Project 1

Sketch, design, implement and document via a web portfolio an animated touch-based and sensor based interface for a mobile phone that is an alternative to 'slide to unlock'.

Acaikei

Acai bowls are colourful smoothie bowls that are popular amongst trendy young people. As a result, they are also heavily customizable, and involve a layer of artistry. To give users a personalizable and artistic unlock experience, we created Acaikei. Users select the combination of topings and drag them onto the acai bowl, creating a beautiful image. The right combination of ingredients unlocks the phone.
