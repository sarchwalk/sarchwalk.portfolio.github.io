The Colour Blender

Assignment 1: Arduino Hello World

Create a simple circuit that uses a button to control an LED.

Client: Gwyneth Paltrow

The Colour Blender is an interactive art installation and game, that tests players knowledge of colour mixing. To achieve any colour in the rainbow, one must either use primary colours on their own, or mix them to achieve secondary colours. To test players knowledge of this task, the Colour Blender prompts the player with a colour and challenges them to recreate it by hitting or jumping on a sequence of buttons. Making colour mixing a fun, active, and mess free activity!

The Colour Blender consists of two lamps, three buttons, and one toggle switch. The larger of the two lamps is used to display a computer generated colour that the player must try to achieve on the smaller lamp. Three buttons, representing red, yellow, and blue, are placed around the room. Once a player presses one of them, the smaller lamp will change to show the colour pertaining to the button. If the right combination of buttons is pressed, the lamps will blink on and off to indicate success. If the wrong combination of buttons is pressed, the user must press the toggle switch to restart.
