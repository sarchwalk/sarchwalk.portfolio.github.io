The Gwyneth Paltrow Button

CPSC 581 - Human Computer Interaction II, Project 0

Sketch, design, implement and document via a web portfolio a software application where the central interaction is with a button, such that the interaction represents some aspect of your client's personality or character.

Client: Gwyneth Paltrow


Gwyneth Paltrow is an Academy Award winning actress and SOMETHING. But as of late has gained notoriety for her lifestlye brand, goop. Gwyneth has maintained her spotlight through her lifestyle company goop, where she promotes non-medically approved treatnents and expensive lifestyle produts. Her recommendations of most concern include vaginal steaming, bee sting facials, and coffee enemas. We chose Gwyneth as our client because we found her unique personality to entertaining, and oddly endearing.
